About

Hello, world! My name is Lydon. Ever since I was a kid, I've loved computers. Back then, I had an old, dusty HP tower that I would routinely strip the parts out of, only to rebuild it right back moments later. After graduating from high school, I trained as a music producer in my home town of Boston at Berklee College of Music. Afterwards, I found mentorship in my craft out in LA.
While I enjoyed making music profusely, and still very much do to this day, my love of tech remained an insatiable itch that I just couldn't scratch. It's for this reason that I've now chosen to pursue a certificate in full-stack web development, and I've created this page to showcase some of my latest projects.
